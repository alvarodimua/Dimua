DIMUÁ

A cinematic pop universe between Spain and Ireland

DIMUÁ is a Spanish artist and songwriter born in Alcoy (Alicante). At the age of 15 he moved to Ireland, where he began developing his craft as a songwriter while immersing himself in Dublin’s creative music scene.

Before launching his solo project, he was the lead singer of the band Clefsico, gaining his first experience performing and recording music in Dublin.

His current project as DIMUÁ has been developed in collaboration with producer Ángel Luján, known for his work with artists such as Alice Wonder, Tulsa, Niños Mutantes, Miranda! and Carlos Ares.

Together they have been building a distinctive musical universe that blends emotional storytelling, atmospheric production and a strong visual identity.

On Friday, March 13th 2026, DIMUÁ released his debut single “Le pedí a la luna.” The track marks the sonic beginning of the entire project and introduces the artistic world that will define his music.

Throughout summer 2026, DIMUÁ will continue releasing new songs that expand this musical universe and reveal the next chapters of his project.
